Development Projects

TripBot - Conversational Interface to Provide Actionable Independent Travel Insight to Blind and Low Vision (BLV) People (Jan. 2024 - Present)

  • Integrated LLMs into a conversational agent providing accurate environmental condition information to BLV individuals, enhancing independent travel insights.
  • Fine-Tuning and leveraged LLaVA to provide continuous instruction and Orientation and Mobility (O&M) aid to BLV individuals, improving their navigation and mobility skills.
  • Conducting pilot testing with BLV people to assess system robustness and fine-tune based on user feedback, using a preference strength algorithm to incorporate BLV individuals’ perceptions of LLM responses.

Investigating the Potential of Immersive Technologies in Spatial Learning for Blind and Low Vision Individuals (Sept. 2023 - Present)

  • Prototyped a VR ping-pong game encouraging play between sighted and BLV individuals, promoting physical activity and cognitive engagement through gameplay.
  • Led the development of dynamic and spatial sound for the virtual ball, based on force, trajectory, and proximity, enhancing the immersive experience.
  • Generated a novel haptic feedback mechanism using a haptic glove, varying intensity and frequency to provide an additional mode of information transmission, improving user interaction.

RLHF Voice-Based Conversational Agent on Intelligent Reflective Surfaces (Mar. 2023 - Jan. 2024)

  • Conceptualized and engineered a smart mirror with a voice agent, pioneering mood analysis via emotion recognition and speech feature analysis using Python, JavaScript, and Node.js, enhancing user emotional well-being.
  • Implemented an RNN model for speech analysis and integrated a novel RLHF algorithm based on POMDP, dynamically responding to user emotions, improving real-time interaction accuracy.
  • Orchestrated the technical creation and empirical research, devising the conversational interface and examining the dynamics between emotion recognition, cognitive engagement, and attention metrics, providing insights for improving user experience.
  • Integrated the Mistral and LLaMA-uncensored model into the emotion agent, catering to a vector space of emotions and providing low-risk advice for mood alleviation, increasing user satisfaction.
  • Created a system with a strong capacity to motivate users succinctly, currently addressing hallucination issues related to hobbies and aiming to eliminate them based on user interaction studies.

IoT-Based Door Locking System in a Smart Living Environment (Mar. 2022 - Dec. 2022)
